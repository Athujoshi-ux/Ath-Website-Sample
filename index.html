My interest in the field of Civil Engineering developed due to my father because he is also a civil engineer and he has always inspired me in choosing this field as a career. During my Higher Secondary Education, my father’s office was on a building site and he sometimes took me there with him. I was extremely fascinated with the way a building is constructed on site and that’s why I decided to pursue bachelors in civil engineering after my Higher Secondary Education

My father served as an Executive Engineer (Civil) for 30 years in Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. (BSNL). My mother is a housewife and she pursue hobbies like painting, sketching and dramatization. Also, she sometimes gives cricket coaching to girls, as she was a former University Level Cricketer. I also have a younger brother who is currently in 12th grade in Shivaji Science College and wants to become a Surgeon.
